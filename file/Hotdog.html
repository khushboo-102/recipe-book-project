Hot-Dog

It is a tasty juck food. It makes very less time.

  • 2 cups cooked- soya chunks/meal maker
  • 100 grams crumbled tofu
  • 2 tsp + 2tbsp oil for pan frying
  • 1 medium size chopped onions
  • 1 green chilli chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder/malli podi
  • 1 tsp cummin powder/jeera podi
  • 1.5 tsp garam masala powder
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 medium size carrots grated
  • 1/2 capsicum/bell pepper chopped
  • wheat bread-1 slice or use 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup carnflour/cornstarch as needed
  1. Take cooked soya chunks in a blender and make it into a mince
  2. Heat oil in a pan, saute onion and chillies with some salt till it is light golden
  3. Add in ginger garlic paste and saute for a minute
  4. Add in all spices powders and give a toss
  5. Now add in minced soya and mix well. Saute for 4 to 5 minutes till the raw smell leaves from soya
  6. Remove this to a bowl and let it cool down.Now crumble wheat bread and tofu mix well with your hands
  7. Now take a portion and roll it into sausage. Do this to the remaining as well.
  8. Now place this in fridge for 30 minutes.
  9. Now roll this in cornflour and put it in a pan with very little oil.Fry this on medium heat till it is golden.
  10. Remove it and set aside. Now take a roll and slit open place the sausage in and drizzle some ketchup over it.Top with onions,tomatoesand capsicum.Server hot.
