Gulgule

It is a very tasty sweetdish. It is a traditional snack Gulgule.Gulgule are traditional Indian sweets and It makes easly.

  • 75 gram jaggery(gur),about 1/2 cup,crumbled or broken up into small pieces
  • 120ml water,about 1/2 cup,hot
  • 120 ml milk,whole milk or full cream milk, about 1/2 cup,warm
  • 230gram whole wheat flour,about 1.5 cups
  • 3/4 teaspoon fennel seeds,crushed
  • 2 green cardamom pods,seeds crushed to make 1/4 teaspoon green cardamom powder,husks discarded
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

Step1

Make jaggery (gur) syrup:

  1. Place crumbled jaggery in a bowl. Pour 120ml hot water (about 1/2cup) over the jaggery followed by the warm milk.Stir or whisk till the jaggery has dissolved completely.Set aside

Step2

Make gulgule batter:

  1. Tip whole wheat flour into a large bowl. Add crushed fennel seeds,green cardamom powder,and baking soda.Stir or whisk to mix well. Pour in the prepared jaggery syrup and use either a ballon whiskor or a wooden spatula to whisk and beat the batter till smooth.Pour in up to 1/4 cup water and mix/beat to get the correct consistency of a thick droppable batter.Cover and set aside for 30minutes.

Step3

Fry the gulgule:

  1. Place a kadhai or work over high heat and pour in enough oil to deep fry the gulgule .While the oil is heating, use a wooden spatula to beat the gulgulebatter to aerate it.Once the oil is hot,reduce the heat to medium.Dip your hand into water to wet it and pick up a walnut-size quantity of batter.Carefuuly drop this into the hot oil.Repeat the process till you have 3 or 4 gulgulefrying in the kadhai,ensuring that you do not crowed the kadhai.Fry, turning as needed till the gulgule are golden brown all over, about 1% minutes.Remove with a slotted spoon, letting oil drip away ,and place on kitchen paper towels so that the excess oilcan be absorbed .

Step4

  1. Repeat in batches till all gulgule batter/dough has been used up.You should be able to make 16 to 20 minute gulgule
