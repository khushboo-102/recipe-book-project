Pav Bhaji

Pav bhaji is very famous dish of Mumbai strret and it makes in less time .

Here's what you need:

  • 400 grms mixed vegetables like potato,cauliflower,carrot,beans,capsicum
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 onion chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomato chapped
  • 50gms peas (shelled)
  • 2 cups hot water
  • 2 green chillies
  • 0.5 teaspoon turmric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon amchur powder
  • 2 tablespoons coriander leaves
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 pinch salt
  1. Firstly we take a cooker. In cooker,boil the mixed vegetables along with peas and 1 cup water in a preesure cooker 1 whistle keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add chopped onions and fry till golden brown. Add ginger garlic paste and saute.
  3. Now add tomatoes,green chillies.and all the spices like turmeric powder, chilli powder,coriander powder,and cumin powder. Fry till the masala is well powder.Fry till the masala is well combined.
  4. Add the cooked vegetables with water,maggi masala-ae-magic,salt as per taste and mix well. Mash the vegetables with a spoon or masher,add amchur powder and mix well
  5. Add the remaining water and simmer till the pavbhaji gravy thickens.Remove from the flame and add lemon juice to zest it up
  6. Garnish the gravy with coriander leaves.
